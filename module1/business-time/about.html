Adventurers at Heart

Here at Willowwood Supply, we don't just serve adventurers: we were founded by one. Owner Donadae Willowwood returned a mere year ago from a century-long exploration of the entire continent, and now he is eager to pass on his stories, expertise, and carefully-chosen goods to those that wish to follow in his footsteps.

Donadae Willowwood

Not a single elf in Summerdale knows more about the outside world than Master Willowwood. From dragons to ghouls to hook horrors to flumphs - he's seen, done battle with, or been slightly irritated by them all. Wondering what you are likely to find in a kobold den and how best to wash off the smell afterward? Donadae knows! Need some advice on the best way to seduce a minotaur? Donadae knows! Trying to decide on the best course of action when the dwarf in your party gets hammered and climbs a massive tree in a haunted forest and can't get back down because he's too dizzy and you're pretty sure you have about five ash wraiths circling the camp? Donadae knows that, too!
